Strawberry Syrup

Description

Use this strawberry syrup to make natural-flavored beverages. This simple recipe came from an attempt to create a more natural substitute for the strawberry syrup sold in stores.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup with sugar
  • 2 cups quartered strawberries

Steps

  1. Combine water and sugar in a saucepan over medium-high heat, stir until sugar is dissolved. Mix strawberries into saucepan and bring to boil, 10 minutes.
  2. Reduce heat to medium-low, simmer until strawberries are mushy and sauce is thick, about 10 minutes. Strain liquid into bottle and refrigerate.
