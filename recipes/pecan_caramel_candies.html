Pecan Caramel Candies

Description

Salty and sweet, these candies are the perfect little snack. Form a kitchen assembly line with your kids, and the making becomes just as fun as the eating.

Ingredients

  • 54 pretzels
  • 54 Rolo candies (about 11 ounces)
  • 54 pecan halves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 250°C. Place pretzels 1 in. apart on foil-lined baking sheets. Top each with a Rolo candy.
  2. Bake 3-4 minutes or until candies are softened (Rolos will still retain their shape). Immediately top with pecans, pressing to spread candy into pretzels. Let stand until set.
